Important Instructions

Please review each point carefully before beginning the assessment.

  1. Time Limit

    You have 45 minutes to complete this assessment. The timer starts immediately when you click "Start Assessment" and cannot be paused.

  2. Question Format

    The assessment consists of multiple-choice questions. Read each question thoroughly before answering.

  3. Scoring System

    Each question is worth 10 points. There is no negative marking for incorrect answers. A minimum score of 70% is required to pass.

  4. Navigation

    You can navigate between questions using the "Next" and "Previous" buttons or the question navigator panel. You can also flag questions to revisit later.

  5. Submission

    Your answers are automatically saved as you progress. Once you complete all questions, click the "Submit" button to finalize your assessment.

  6. Academic Integrity

    This assessment must be completed without assistance from others. Any form of cheating will result in disqualification and potential disciplinary action.
